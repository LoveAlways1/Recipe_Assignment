How to Prepare Black Soup

Decription:Black soup also known as omoebe is a Nigerian soup made mainly from three mail leaves namely scent leaf, uziza leaf and bitter leaf. Some other people would use more leaves which includes ugwu, utazi etc. Black Soup is a native soup of Benin people but more popular in Edo State.

Recipe Information

  • Preparation Time: 30 mins
  • Number of servings: 3 and above
  • Difficulty: Beginner friendly

Ingredients

  • Palm kernel extract
  • Protein of choice (snail, beef, chicken, shrimps, smoked fish, pormo etc.)
  • Crayfish
  • Utazi leaf
  • Washed Bitterleaf
  • Uziza leaf
  • Scent leaf
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Pepper
  • Onion

Prepation Steps

  1. Pour your palm kernel extract in a pot
  2. Add your diced onion, protein of choice, a generous amount of crayfish, Pepper, salt & some seasoning
  3. Cover the pot and leave it to cook for 5 mins
  4. Add your blended leaves (leaves listed in the Ingredients above)
  5. Cover the pot and allow to cook for about 15minutes
  6. Once soup thickens, and changes color to a dark green/black, it's ready.

    7. Additional Information: You can use palm oil in place of palm kernel, the soup will still come out as nice
